Web Development

Starting with basic HTML and CSS during my early undergrad years, I found myself eventually
participating in a full-stack development apprenticeship one week out of graduation. Agile
full-stack software development, intensive hands-on projects, and collaborative programming
became my day to day. Visually appealing designs, subtle details and brand guidelines combined
to innovative interfaces across various touch points became my daily companion. Out of this
interest for development, my passion for functionality and structure evolved. Jumping right into
Photoshop to create something didn’t feel accurate anymore and skipping the steps of building
a framework based on functionality and usability became inevitable.
