CLP/PLP Redesign, Hypothesis & Challenges

By adding a more interactive filtering system, larger images, and infinite scroll the user will be more likely to stay on the site longer and consequently more likely to make a purchase.

Shipping Threshold, Hypothesis

By adding a more interactive filtering system, larger images, and infinite scroll the user will be more likely to stay on the site longer and consequently more likely to make a purchase.

Quick Look Redesign, Hypothesis

Reorganizing the quick look modal will provide a more smooth user experience and therefore encourage customers to stay on the site to browse longer.

Positive Reviews, Hypothesis

By pulling a five star review, which are placed in a way that the user has to find them, and displaying it at the top of the CLP the user will feel more confident and their choice and therefore more likely to add to bag.

Impulse Shade, Hypothesis

By presenting three trending products to the customer on the mobile cart page, the customer will be more likely to add these items to their cart. This will increase conversion and percentage of items added to cart.
